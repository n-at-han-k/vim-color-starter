INTRODUCTION                                    *color-starter* *vim-color-starter*

A colorscheme starter template.

USAGE                                           *color-starter-usage*

Enable the colorscheme: >
    colorscheme color-starter
<

ANSI COLOR MAPPING                              *color-starter-colors*

The colorscheme uses these ANSI color indices:
>
    0: Black            8: Bright Black (dark gray)
    1: Red              9: Bright Red
    2: Green           10: Bright Green
    3: Yellow          11: Bright Yellow
    4: Blue            12: Bright Blue
    5: Magenta         13: Bright Magenta
    6: Cyan            14: Bright Cyan
    7: White (gray)    15: Bright White
<

REQUIREMENTS                                    *color-starter-requirements*

- Terminal with ANSI color support
- 'termguicolors' must be off (set automatically)

NOTES                                           *color-starter-notes*

- Works best with standard ANSI color palettes
- Non-standard schemes (e.g. base16) may look odd
- Neovim-specific groups are conditionally applied
